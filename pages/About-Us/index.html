About «Project»

Why the project was created «KGVIS»?

project «KGVIS» was created in order to improve tourism in the city of Karakol, as well as to improve orientation in the city.

How it works?

Everything is very simple, the User needs to choose where he wants to go and how to get there. Our simple site will help him with this.

Also on the site you can go to our telegram bot.

you can go to the bot by clicking on the "Interesting" button

What can a bot do?

The bot can show the weather and temperature in the city.
